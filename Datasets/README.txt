This directory contains datasets used in the programs.

The NIBRS Master Files can be obtained from the FBI Crime Data Explorer, https://cde.ucr.cjis.gov/. 
These files are subject to change; the Master Files used in the paper were downloaded in May, 2025.

Data files ACSDP1Y2021.DP05 and ACSSPP1Y2021.S0201, used in computing the demographic variables for the imputation program, are available for download from data.census.gov.

PHX_count.csv:  Monthly crime counts for Phoenix in 2021
Source: https://www.phoenix.gov/content/dam/phoenix/policesite/documents/crime-stats-and-maps/
2021_ucr_monthly.pdf, downloaded May 20, 2025

Tempe_count.csv:  Monthly crime counts for Tempe in 2021
Source: https://www.tempe.gov/government/police/crime-statistics-reports, downloaded April 26, 2023

Tucson_count.csv:  Monthly crime counts for Tucson in 2021
Source: Calculated from the database https://policeanalysis.tucsonaz.gov/pages/reported-crimes, downloaded May 27, 2025.
